Questions

Does this work on all eBay sites?
Yes — ebay.com, ebay.co.uk, ebay.de, ebay.com.au, and all other regional eBay sites.
Will it slow down my browsing?
No. The extension runs locally in your browser and processes listings in under 300ms. You won't notice any difference in page load times.
What data do you collect?
None. Zero. The extension runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.
Can I still see the filtered listings?
Yes — toggle grey-out mode and filtered listings appear faded with a warning badge. You can always click through to view them if you choose.
Why are classified ads dangerous?
eBay classified ads don't go through eBay's checkout system. There's no buyer protection, no money-back guarantee, and no dispute resolution. Scammers use them to collect payment outside eBay's system.
